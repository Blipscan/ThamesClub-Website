Your Home in the City

More room to live, meet, work, dine, and belong — in the heart of New London.

Not everything you need fits inside your home.

The Thames Club offers members a gracious city address beyond their front door: rooms for conversation, quiet corners for reading or work, dining and events, and a familiar place to gather before or after the day’s obligations.

For those whose lives are increasingly compact, mobile, and busy, the Club offers something rare: more living space, more connection, and a deeper place in the city.

Space

Rooms to meet, read, talk, host, pause, and feel at home in New London.

Access

Belonging

A place where members are known, welcomed, and part of a continuing tradition.
